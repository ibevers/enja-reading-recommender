Ascension at night
Dairiki Konno

For the lazy rhythm of the wall clock
To the light of a sleepy Western light
Our life sinking deeply
Listen to 9 o'clock and listen to 10 o'clock
Everything is when you want to sleep
The sighs on the bottom of everyone become bubbles
The heart of ascension
Climbing with my soul
