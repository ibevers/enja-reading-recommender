Vague stratification tears the melody on the ground
I care if your health can tolerate it
In these cases you're too classic to go crazy
And your E line stops ringing alone
I tried to stop ringing until it rotted --- was it just my misery?
In a difficult, vague stratification, you reconnected the E line again
A stringless koto that penetrates all other underground rays beyond infrared, ultraviolet and green
We / \ sought so much to declare that they would play within themselves
It's easy to say I --- but you --- he hesitated
You're even easier to say, but I'm --- and the other guys mumbled
In such an era, when you still bet your art on the old-fashioned E-line
People's poetry will wield its instrument to a new key as it conquers the nabati.
And not behind some sort of accidental hidden musical score
When your own E-line is produced at the "People's Poetry Factory", where you are a worker engineer with a silent operation and a calm passion ...
